# Power BI Superstore Report

📊 Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product categories, and operational efficiency. Built using historical retail transaction data, the dashboard enables stakeholders such as business managers, sales analysts, and operations teams to make data-driven decisions by visualizing key metrics, trends, and performance drivers across regions, segments, and time periods.

The dashboard integrates executive-level KPIs with detailed customer, product, and logistics insights, offering a holistic view of retail business performance.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze overall sales, profit, and profit margin performance
Track year-over-year (YoY) growth in sales and profit
Identify top-performing regions, states, and cities
Evaluate customer segments and their contribution to revenue
Assess product category and sub-category profitability
Understand the impact of discounts and delivery modes on profit
Monitor operational efficiency using delivery timelines

📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)

Total Sales: $2.30M
Total Profit: $286K
Profit Margin: 12.5%
Customer Count: 793
Orders Count: 5,009
Sales per Customer: ~$2.9K
Average Discount: 15.62%
Average Delivery Time: 3.96 days
These KPIs provide a high-level snapshot of business health and efficiency. 


📈 Dashboard Insights
1️⃣ Sales & Profit Performance

Strong YoY growth with 46.88% increase in sales and 48.42% increase in profit
Profit margin remains stable around 12–13%, indicating controlled cost structures

2️⃣ Regional Performance

West Region leads with 31% of total sales
California is the most profitable state with $76.5K profit
Los Angeles is the top city by sales (~$155K)

3️⃣ Customer Segment Analysis

Consumer Segment dominates total sales
Corporate Segment is the best-performing segment, contributing $706K (34%)
Home Office contributes smaller but consistent revenue

4️⃣ Product Category & Sub-Category Insights

Technology is the top product category, driving $717K (37%) of sales
Copiers, Phones, and Accessories generate the highest profits
Labels, Paper, and Envelopes show the highest profit margins despite lower sales volumes

5️⃣ Discount & Profitability Analysis

High discounts do not always translate into higher profits
Sub-categories like Binders and Copiers receive heavy discounts, impacting margins
Optimal discount strategies can significantly improve profitability

6️⃣ Shipping & Operations

Standard Class shipping accounts for the highest sales and profit
Same-Day delivery has minimal contribution, indicating limited demand
Average delivery time of ~4 days reflects efficient logistics operations

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Microsoft Power BI – Dashboard development and interactive visualizations
Superstore Dataset – Retail transactional data
Data Modeling & Measures – DAX calculations for KPIs and YoY metrics
Data Transformation – Cleaning, aggregation, and filtering

Visual Analytics – Maps, KPI cards, bar charts, line charts, and tables.
